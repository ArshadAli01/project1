An adventure is an exciting or unusual experience that often involves taking risks. It can include activities such as exploring new places, trying new things, or facing physical or mental challenges

Adventures can be planned or spontaneous, and they can happen alone or with others. They can be big or small, and they can take place in remote locations or in your own backyard.

para gliding

Paragliding is a form of recreational and competitive aviation in which a person rides a small, motorless parachute-like wing, called a paraglider, to fly through the air. It is considered a form of foot-launched powered hang gliding.

Paragliders are designed to be lightweight and easy to transport, and can be launched from a variety of locations, including hillsides, mountains, and even from the back of a vehicle.

Surfing

Surfing is the sport of riding waves in an upright or prone position. Surfers catch the ocean, river, or man-made waves and glide across the surface of the water until they break and lose their energy.
